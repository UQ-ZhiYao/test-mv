Our Mandate

We compound capital the way a careful owner would — concentrating in a handful of businesses we understand, holding them through cycles, and treating every ringgit as our own.

The Approach

A repeatable process, not a forecast

Consistent active management since December 2021 — across multiple market cycles.

01

Underwrite

We study a business until we can explain its economics simply. Quality of the franchise comes before the price we'll pay.

02

Concentrate

We typically hold fewer than twenty positions, sized by conviction. Diversification for its own sake dilutes our best ideas.

03

Hold & review

Every position is re-underwritten each quarter. We act on facts that change the thesis — not on market noise.
